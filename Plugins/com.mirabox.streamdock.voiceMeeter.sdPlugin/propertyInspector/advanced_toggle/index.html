Mode1 is used to turn things ON. Mode 2 is used to turn things OFF. Example: Lets use Mode1 to turn Mono on (and do aditional things): "Strip[0].mono=1;Bus[2].Gain=-20;" Now "Mode1 Check" should be: "Strip[0].mono" Mode2 should turn mono Off: "Strip[0].mono=0;Bus[2].Gain=-10;..."

* NEW: Mode1 Check now supports AND/OR: 'Strip[0].mono AND Strip[1].B1' Additional examples at https://download.vb-audio.com/Download_CABLE/VoicemeeterRemoteAPI.pdf
